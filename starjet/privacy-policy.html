Privacy Policy

Star Jet Business Management System

1. Introduction

Welcome to Star Jet's Privacy Policy. This document explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our business management system. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data.

Effective Date: December 2024
Last Updated: December 2024

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect the following personal information:

  • Account Information: Email address, name, role (admin, salesman, accountant)
  • Business Data: Sales records, expense reports, financial transactions
  • Usage Data: Login times, system interactions, feature usage
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information

2.2 How We Collect Information

  • Direct input when you create an account or use our services
  • Automatic collection through cookies and similar technologies
  • System logs and analytics data
  • Communication with our support team

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To provide and maintain our business management services
  • Authentication: To verify your identity and manage access to the system
  • Role-Based Access: To provide appropriate features based on your role
  • Business Operations: To process sales, expenses, and financial data
  • Security: To protect against fraud, abuse, and security threats
  • Improvement: To enhance our services and user experience
  • Communication: To send important updates and notifications

4. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Role-based access with strict authentication
  • Rate Limiting: Protection against brute force attacks
  • CSRF Protection: Cross-site request forgery prevention
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Secure Infrastructure: Hosted on secure, compliant platforms

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party services that help us operate our system (e.g., hosting providers, database services)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • Consent: With your explicit consent for specific purposes

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Request data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to certain processing activities
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent where applicable

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services to you
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements
  • Maintain business records

When we no longer need your information, we will securely delete or anonymize it.

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain your login session
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze system usage and performance
  • Enhance security and prevent fraud

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed and stored in countries other than your own. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and implement appropriate safeguards.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our website
  • Sending email notifications to registered users
  • Displaying prominent notices in the application

Your continued use of our services after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
